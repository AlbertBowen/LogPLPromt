    Installation

    Disable Secure Boot in the BIOS to boot from a USB stick.

    Touchpad

    The touchpad which identifies as ELAN0674:00 04F3:3193 Touchpad is not very responsive. Sometimes the cursor jumps randomly when trying to perform a tap. This can be verified with https://no-cursor.com/, taking 60 seconds to complete the test.

    Removing the psmouse module seems to improve the touchpad. After this, the trackpoint and the physical touchpad buttons stop working but the touchpad becomes more usable; it now only takes 30 seconds to complete the above test.

    The fix for IdeaPad 3 14IIL05 may also work.
