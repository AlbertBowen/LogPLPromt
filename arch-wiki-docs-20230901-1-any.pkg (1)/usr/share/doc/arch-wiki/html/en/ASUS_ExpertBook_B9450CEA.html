    This article applies to B9450CEA-XH75 (16G RAM/1TB SSD) and B9450CEA-XH77 (32G RAM/2TB SSD).

    Summary

    The 2nd generation of ASUS ExpertBook B9450 with 11th Gen Intel CPU with Intel Xe graphics.

    Models are B9450CEA-* compared to B9450FA-* of the 1st generation.

    BIOS

    To boot from a USB stick, you may have to turn off Fast Boot and Secure Boot: How to boot the system from USB flash drive/CD-ROM.

    Warning: Even though the model line is B9450CEA, in BIOS and on the official support page it is indicated as B9400CEA.

    dmidecode will also display Product Name as ASUS EXPERTBOOK B9400CEA_B9450CEA and BIOS Version as, e.g., B9400CEA.301.

    Audio

    This laptop requires firmware in order for the soundcard to work. See Advanced Linux Sound Architecture#ALSA firmware.

    Suspend mode

    See Power management/Suspend and hibernate#Changing suspend method.
