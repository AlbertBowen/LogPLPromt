    Dell XPS early 2020 edition.

    Installation

    In some units, the NVMe drive is configured to operate in RAID mode by default. This may prevent the bootloader from finding your boot disk. In order to fix this, press 2 to enter the Dell UEFI. Go to Storage > NVMe Operation and make sure that Select AHCI/NVMe mode is selected.

    Video

    Take a look at XPS 13 (9310) Video drivers (Talk) if you encounter any screen artifacts in qt5 programs when using mesa instead of xf86-video-intel.

    Audio

    Unlike the Dell XPS 13 (9310), this laptop's audio appears to work out-of-the-box, or at least using linux-firmware.

    Fingerprint sensor

    The fingerprint sensor in this device is not supported by the binary driver from the Dell XPS 13 (9310).
